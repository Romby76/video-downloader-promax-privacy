# Privacy Policy for Video Downloader ProMax

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Video Downloader ProMax ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our Google Chrome extension.

## 1. Information We Collect and Process
* **License Verification Data**: When you activate the extension, we collect and process the license key you provide and a randomly generated unique installation identifier (UUID). This data is used solely to verify your subscription status and enforce the limit of 2 active devices per license.
* **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or payment details. Payment processing is handled securely by our third-party provider (Lemon Squeezy), and we never have access to your financial information.

## 2. Web Browsing and Media Detection
* **Local Processing**: While the extension monitors network requests to detect video streams (HLS, DASH, MP4), all media detection, downloading, decryption, and assembly happen **100% locally** inside your web browser. 
* **No Tracking**: We do not track, log, collect, or transmit your web browsing history, the URLs of the web pages you visit, or the videos you download.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties
* We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. We do not share any data except for the sole purpose of validating your software license with our licensing server.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
