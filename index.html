Does the extension work on educational platforms like Hotmart?
Yes, fully! The extension includes a specialized background parser designed to capture and sequentially download video lections directly from Hotmart and other standard educational platforms.
How does the 2-device license limit work?
When you buy a license key, you can activate it on up to two different browsers or PCs simultaneously (for example, your work laptop and your home desktop). If you need to switch devices, you can deactivate a device or contact support.
Is the video stitching done in the cloud?
No! Everything is processed locally in your browser. We don't upload your videos to any external cloud, ensuring maximum speed, zero queue wait times, and complete privacy of your content.
